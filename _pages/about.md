I am a final year BPharm Student at the School of Pharmacy, BRAC University, Bangladesh. I have a strong research interest in Medicinal Chemistry, Cheminformatics and Drug Discovery. I want to devote myself trying to solve some of the most challenging problems of medical science by incorporating the knowledge of chemistry, pharmacology and computer science thus making an impact on this world and leaving it a better place.

Research
======
I am currently working on antimicrobial resistance, more specifically I am trying to find potential compound that can block bacterial efflux pumps which are responsible for transporting antibiotics and other toxic compounds, eventually contributing to antibiotic resistance.
